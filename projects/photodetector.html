Photodetector

High-power, low-noise measurements at >10 MHz bandwidth

Overview

This custom single-board computer was developed as a compact platform for control and data acquisition in a larger quantum photonic system. It integrates high-speed IO, embedded control logic, and software-defined calibration modules.

Design & Implementation

The design process included schematic capture, PCB layout in Altium, firmware co-design with FPGA developers, and integration testing. We focused on signal integrity, low latency control, and compact thermal design.

Results & Deployment

The board was validated in lab conditions and deployed in a high-stability rack-mounted environment. Performance exceeded spec with
10x improved calibration time and 50% footprint reduction compared to prior solutions.
